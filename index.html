About me

Hi there! I'm Pablo and I am learning to be a web developer. I come from Spain, but moved around when I was a kid, including Brussels, Madrid and Berlin. I finished school in Madrid and decided to move to Berlin to study civil engineering.

Fast-forward ten years: I have a BSc in civil engineering but work as an admissions manager at ESMT Berlin, an international business school. Having always had a hunger for tech and a deep curiosity for how things work, I begin looking for a way to shift my career into something technical. The Coding Bootcamp seems like a perfect match.

And here I am - two months into the bootcamp and enjoying every day of it. As I progress through the program, you too should be able to see my progress reflected on this portfolio page.

If you want to reach me, you can do so on GitHub or LinkedIn.
